Discover Tours

Iceland is one of the Nordic countries, located between the Atlantic Ocean and the Arctic Circle, and it is a country full of natural scenery. It is worth exploring. Before traveling, it is recommended to plan your itinerary in advance and prepare for the trip in order to enjoy it better. Here are some introductions and suggestions for traveling to Iceland.

view

Land

Natural landscapes: Iceland has many natural landscapes, including glaciers, volcanoes, waterfalls, hot springs, and Northern Lights, etc. The most famous attractions include the Golden Waterfall, Glacier Lagoon, Geysir volcano, Danish Bay, and the Blue Lagoon, etc.

Experience Activities: In Iceland, you can participate in various outdoor activities such as hiking, horseback riding, fishing, snorkeling, diving, and skiing, etc. Additionally, you can also spend a night in camping and ice houses, or relax in hot springs.

Food and Beverage: Traditional Icelandic food includes fish, lamb, horse meat, and whale meat, etc. There is also a special local beverage called "brenniv√≠n," which is a type of potato-based schnapps. Additionally, you can also taste various modern international cuisine.

Seasonal Selection: Iceland's climate changes rapidly, and you need to choose different seasons for travel based on your preferences and schedule. The summer has warm weather, which is suitable for hiking and exploring natural landscapes, while the winter is suitable for viewing the Northern Lights and glacier exploration.

Transportation: Public transportation in Iceland is not very developed, so it is best to rent a car or join a tour group. Additionally, you can also take buses or flights to different cities and attractions.
